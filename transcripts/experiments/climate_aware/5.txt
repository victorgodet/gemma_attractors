*   **Macroeconomic Impacts:** Climate change can disrupt economic activity, leading to lower growth, higher inflation, and financial instability.
*   **Systemic Shocks:** The interconnectedness of the financial system means that a shock in one sector or region can quickly spread to others, leading to a systemic crisis.
